Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the upcoming webinar?
You can register by filling out the registration form on our website. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with the webinar details.
Will I receive a reminder before the webinar starts?
Yes! We will send you reminder emails before the webinar, including a final reminder on the day of the session.
Can I ask questions during the webinar?
Absolutely! There will be a live Q&A session where you can ask the speaker questions. You can also drop your queries in the chat box.
How can I access the recording of a past webinar?
If you registered for the webinar, you will receive an email with the recording link. If you haven't registered, you can request access by filling out the request form on our website.
Who can I contact if I have trouble accessing the recording?
If you're facing any issues, please reach out to our support team at support@Grad Certify.com for assistance.

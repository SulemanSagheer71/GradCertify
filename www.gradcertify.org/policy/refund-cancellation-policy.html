Refund/Cancellation Policy

Grad Certify Education Services Private Limited

Introduction

At Grad Certify Education Services Private Limited, we are committed to delivering high-quality online courses and excellent customer service. We understand that there may be instances where a customer needs to request a cancellation. Our refund policy is designed to handle such requests in a fair and timely manner.

Refund Eligibility

Refund requests will be considered only within 7 days of the class starting date.

To qualify for a refund, the following criteria must also be met:

  • 1. You have not attended more than two live sessions.
  • 2. You have not submitted any assignments or project work.
  • 3. You have not earned a certificate of completion for the course.

Note: Some courses may have specific refund policies or additional terms and conditions. These will be clearly stated on the respective course page.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our Customer Support Team by emailing support@Grad Certify.com. Include the following information in your email:

  • 1. Your full name and email address used for the purchase
  • 2. The name of the course for which you are requesting a refund.
  • 3. A brief reason for the refund request.

Our team will review your request and respond within 5 business days. If your refund is approved, it will be processed as soon as possible. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been initiated.

Please note: It may take up to 10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or credit card company’s policies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this refund policy, please feel free to contact us: support@Grad Certify.com.

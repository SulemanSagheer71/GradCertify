Privacy Policy

Grad Certify Education Services Private Limited

Introduction

At Grad Certify EDUCATION SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our user's data. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your rights and serves to inform you about:

  • ● The types of information we collect
  • ● How we use this information
  • ● The rights and choices you have about your information
  • ● Measures we take to keep your information safe

Our practices comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA), European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

What Information We Collect

We collect several types of information, including:

  • ● Personal Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other personal data you choose to provide us.
  • ● Usage Information: this includes information about how and when you use our website and services.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • ● Provide, improve, and customize our services
  • ● Communicate with you
  • ● Ensure the security and integrity of our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Retention of Your Information

We keep your information for no longer than necessary for the purposes for which it is processed. The length of time for which we retain information depends on the purposes for which we collected and use it and/or as required to comply with applicable laws.

Log Files

Grad Certify follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any personally identifiable information. The purpose of the information is for analysing trends, administering the site, tracking users and movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Cookies

We use cookies on our website to provide a more personalized and efficient experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site's owners. You can manage and delete cookies through your browser settings.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may, from time to time, review and update this Privacy Policy, including new laws, regulations, practices and technology. Any changes to this Privacy Policy may be advised to you by updating this page on our Website. We will aim to provide reasonable advance notice of such changes though this may not always be possible depending on the circumstances. We encourage you to check this page from time to time for any changes.
